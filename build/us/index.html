8 GREAT reasons to invest in the UK

The UK is ready for business - are you?

  1. Business is booming

    The UK is the 5th largest economy in the world, with over 420,000 new businesses launching in 2015 alone

  2. In good company

    Many of the biggest and most dynamic companies choose the UK. Coca-Cola, Honda and Sony all have their European headquarters here

  3. A global financial hub

    London was rated as the world’s leading financial centre, with the London Stock Exchange hosting 3,100 companies from over 70 countries

  4. A gateway to the world

    Positioned between East and West, the UK’s location and time zone makes it easy to do business across American, European and Asian markets

  5. Pioneering innovation

    With renowned R&D capabilities, the UK is ranked 2nd in the Global Innovation Index

  6. Educational excellence

    Home to 4 out of the global top 8 universities, the UK is bursting with world-class talent and ideas

  7. A pro-business tax system

    The UK is renowned for its low-burden, competitive tax system - including the lowest corporation tax rates in the G7 and the joint lowest in the G20

  8. Skills for the 21st century

    The UK ranks second only to the USA among the powerhouse economies for growing, retaining and attracting talent

The UK investment guide

You’re thinking about bringing your business to the UK. Here’s our quick guide to the important steps you need to get set up

  1. Establishing a UK Adress

    You’ll need this for opening a bank account and for setting up your business.

  2. Apply for a visa

    The UK offers a wide range of individual and business visas so you can find the ones that work for you. The application process can take up to three months,so plan early OC testing.

  3. The process

    Setting up a UK business bank account will make it easy for your company to operate in the UK. Just bear in mind that the process can take up to three months and you may need to travel.

  5. Key business taxes

    The UK has one of the most competitive tax systems in the world - check out how the rates and regulations can work for you.

  7. A renowned legal system

    With strong intellectual property laws and fair procurement procedures, the UK legal system is a globally renowned institution.
